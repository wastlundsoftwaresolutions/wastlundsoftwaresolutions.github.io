Privacy Policy for Starfish Trivia

Last updated: June 18, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Starfish Trivia application (the "App"). At Wastlund Software Solutions ("we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you when you use our App. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

Information Stored Locally

The App stores some data locally on your device to enhance your user experience. This data includes:

  • High Scores: To track your best performance in each trivia category.
  • Seen Questions: To ensure you receive new questions in subsequent quizzes.

This information is stored exclusively on your device using standard application storage and is never transmitted to us or any third party. You have full control over this data, and it can be cleared by deleting the App from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
